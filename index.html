MY FAVORITE FOOD

Hello there, my name is Ava and I'm so pleased to have you on this page

Today I'd like to tell you how to prepare my favorite food, which is an African delicacy called Jollof Rice

This jollof rice has been a favorite food of mine from time immemorial, I can't really say why, but I guess it's because of the way my mum prepares it, sometimes she adds this locust beans to it which makes me love it even more and I really hope you'd love it too!

Below is the list of ingredients needed in preparing the dish and the detailed steps highlighted after, Enjoy!

Ingredients Needed

  • Two or three cups of parboiled rice
  • Groundnut oil
  • Onion bulb
  • Tomato paste
  • Bell peppper
  • Salt and seasoning
  • Spices
    1. Crayfish
    2. Cow liver
    3. Dried fish

The Making

  1. Wash the rice with clean water
  2. Dice the onion bulb

    🔪

  3. Rinse the bell paper
      4. The bell pepper can be blended with the crayfish using a manual blender

      Please if you do not like peppery food do not include the bell pepper

      🙅

  4. Pour a cup of groundnut oil into the cooking pot and put stove on medium heat
  5. Allow to heat for 2 minutes the pour the diced onions
  6. Allow to fry for a minute or so then add the tomato paste, grinded bell pepper and crayfish with the seasong and remaining spices
  7. Allow to cook for 6 minutes

    😀

  8. Pour the washed rice and stir
  9. Add reasonble quantity of water, cover the pot and allow to cook till the rice is soft
  10. The above step should take about 20 minutes

    ⏱
  11. The food is now ready, dish out and Enjoy

    😃
